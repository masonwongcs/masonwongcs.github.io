ABOUT US

HISTORY

Our Company was incorporated in Malaysia on 26 January 2010, as a private limited company under the name ManagePay Systems Sdn Bhd (“ManagePay”). It was listed on Ace Market of Bursa Malaysia on 15 March 2011. The Group’s principle business activities are now carried out under its three (3) wholly-owned subsidiaries, which were acquired on 23 March 2010:

1. ManagePay Services Sdn Bhd (“MPSB”)
formerly known as Multimedia Prospect Sdn Bhd
2. ManagePay Technologies Sdn Bhd (“MTSB”)
formerly known as Sina Technologies Sdn Bhd, incorporated in August 2000
3. ManagePay Marketing Sdn Bhd (“MMSB”)
formerly known as Whatdevice (Malaysia) Sdn Bhd, incorporated in August 2000

Three (3) new wholly-owned subsidiaries were formed on 2 Nov 2012, namely:

1. ManagePay Cards Sdn Bhd (“MCSB”)
2. ManagePay Resources Sdn Bhd (“MRSB”)
3. ManagePay Innovation Sdn Bhd (“MISB”)
