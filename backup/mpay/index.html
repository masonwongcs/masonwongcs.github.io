ABOUT US

ManagePay Systems Berhad is a Malaysia-based investment holding company. The Company operates through two segments: Payment services and Non-payment services. The Company's Payment services segment is involved in terminal services, third party acquiring, payment services, business outsourcing services and loyalty management services. The Company's Non-payment services segment is involved in the software security, information communications and technology services, and trading of gadgets. The Company's subsidiaries include ManagePay Services Sdn. Bhd., ManagePay Technologies Sdn. Bhd., ManagePay Marketing Sdn. Bhd., ManagePay Cards Sdn. Bhd., ManagePay Innovation Sdn. Bhd. and ManagePay Resources Sdn. Bhd., among others.
